Our Vision

To become a church that comes together in unity fo the expansion of God's kingdom on this earth! We hold strongly to the words written in Matthew 6:10 "Your kingdom come, your will be done on earth as it is in heaven" & Psalm 91:1 "He who dwells in the shelter of the most high will abide in the shadow of the almighty", that heaven may come to invade the earth as we encounter Jesus in our Secret Place.

To see the kingdom of God advance through passionate people who love God and love others.
